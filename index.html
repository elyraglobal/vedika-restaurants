001 / Our Story

Tradition Meets Table

For over 15 years, Vedika has served Jammu authentic, 100% pure-vegetarian food made with premium ingredients, traditional methods, and pure desi ghee. Today we welcome you at two warm locations — Miran Sahib and Karan Bagh.

From our signature momos to rich gravies and freshly made sweets, every plate reflects our promise: pure, authentic, unforgettable. Every guest is treated like family.
